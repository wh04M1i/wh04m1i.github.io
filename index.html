About Me

Hello! I'm Goverdhan Kumar, a cybersecurity engineer deeply passionate about securing the digital world and empowering others through knowledge. I specialize in web application security, digital forensics, and OSINT. With a background rooted in ethical hacking and security education, I believe in demystifying cyber concepts for students and professionals alike.

I love conducting awareness sessions, solving forensic challenges, reading self-help books, and converting ideas into action. I come from Patna, Bihar and currently reside in Noida, Uttar Pradesh — always exploring new ways to bridge cybersecurity with real-world impact.

Whether it's hosting a CTF, sketching, building security tools, or leading awareness campaigns — I'm a self-learner, public speaker, mentor, and a lifelong explorer.

I'm also the co-founder of three growing ventures that reflect my passions:

  • Foxfoster – A platform offering freelancing services, blogs, and digital content. Through Foxfoster, I aim to share practical knowledge and empower learners through educational tools and awareness initiatives.
  • The Art Act – A personal brand focused on sketching and digital art. I create hand-drawn and digital illustrations, blending creativity with cybersecurity awareness.
  • Vulnshields Consulting Pvt. Ltd. – A cybersecurity consulting company delivering VAPT, training, and awareness services to secure digital environments and empower organizations.

Each of these reflects my mission to connect knowledge with creativity and bring real change through digital empowerment. Welcome to my personal portfolio — a space where security, learning, and expression converge.
